Prosecco & elderflower cocktail


Ingredients :

  • 1. 8 edible flowers (such as violets or rose petals)
  • 2. 200ml cold jasmine tea
  • 3. 1 tsp orange blossom honey
  • 4. 25ml elderflower cordial
  • 5. juice 1 grapefruit
  • 6. 75ml vodka
  • 7. 300ml prosecco
  • 8. extra ice cubes
How to make :

  1. 1. Before the Contest, freeze the 8 edible flowers in 8 ice cubes, if you like.
  2. 2. Pour the jasmine tea into a cocktail shaker with the orange blossom honey, elderflower cordial, grapefruit juice and vodka. Add a handful of (normal) ice cubes and shake well.
  3. 3. Strain into 4 tall glasses. Divide the Prosecco between the glasses, then add extra ice and the flowery ice cubes (if using).
