Avocado smoothie



Ingredients :
  • Â½ avocado Avocado, peeled, stoned and roughly chopped
  • generous handful spinach
  • generous handful kale, washed well
  • 50g Pineapple chunks
  • 10cm piece cucumber, roughly chopped
  • 300ml coconut water
How to make :
  1. Put the avocado, spinach, kale, pineapple and cucumber in the blender.
  2. Top up with coconut water, then blitz until smooth.
