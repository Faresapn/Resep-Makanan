Low-sugar lime & basil green juice


Ingredients :
  • 70ml chilled apple and elderflower juice
  • 50g baby spinach
  • 20g basil leaves
  • 6cm piece of cucumber (about 100g), chopped
How to make :

  1. Pour the apple juice into a large jug then add the spinach, basil, cucumber, lime and 100ml chilled water.
  2. Blitz really well with a hand blender until very smooth. Pour into a glass and drink straightaway.
